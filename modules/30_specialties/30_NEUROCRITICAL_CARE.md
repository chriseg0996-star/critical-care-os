# 30_NEUROCRITICAL_CARE

## Scope

Use for SAH, TBI, stroke, ICH, ICP, EVD, seizures, EEG and TCD.

## Priorities

- Airway and oxygenation
- Cerebral perfusion pressure
- ICP control
- Secondary brain injury prevention
- Seizure control
- Sodium/osmolar strategy
- Vasospasm surveillance
- DVT prevention and bleeding risk
- Rehabilitation and functional prognosis

## Scores

Calculate when possible:
- Fisher / modified Fisher
- WFNS
- Hunt-Hess
- GRAEB
- NIHSS
- ASPECTS
- ICH score
