# 12_ICU_DISCHARGE_NOTES

## Scope

Use for ICU discharge / transfer notes.

## Required sections

- Header
- Admission diagnosis
- ICU course
- Major interventions
- Current status by systems
- Pending studies
- Recommendations
- Prognosis
- Signatures

## Style

Focus on what the receiving service needs.
Avoid excessive historical detail.
Emphasize functional status, airway, nutrition, antimicrobials, renal support, wounds/drains, and follow-up needs.
