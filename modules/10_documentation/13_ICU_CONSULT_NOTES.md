# 13_ICU_CONSULT_NOTES

## Scope

Use for:
- ICU consultation
- response to interconsultation
- OR evaluation
- non-ICU admission recommendation
- unavailable patient at time of evaluation

## Required structure

1. Header
2. Reason for consult
3. Relevant clinical context
4. Current illness
5. Physical examination by systems
6. Paraclinical studies
7. Imaging
8. Comentario
9. Recommendations
10. Signatures

## Comentario

Use integrated clinical reasoning and disposition.
Avoid wording that sounds unlike the user.
