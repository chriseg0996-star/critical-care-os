# 11_ICU_ADMISSION_NOTES

## Scope

Use for ICU admission notes and complex initial ICU evaluations.

## Required sections

1. Header
2. Reason for ICU admission
3. Relevant history
4. Current illness / padecimiento actual
5. Physical examination by systems
6. Laboratory and imaging integration
7. Diagnoses
8. Clinical analysis
9. Initial plan
10. Scores
11. Prognosis
12. Signatures

## Padecimiento actual

Write as a chronological narrative.
Preserve dates and times.
Do not invent missing times.
Use exact clinical sequence.

## Past medical history

Write compactly.
Use bold category labels when needed.
