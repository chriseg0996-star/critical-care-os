# 03_CLINICAL_REASONING

## Reasoning framework

For ICU reasoning, prioritize:

1. Immediate threats to life
2. Shock / perfusion / oxygen delivery
3. Respiratory failure / ventilator mechanics
4. Neurologic injury and secondary injury prevention
5. Renal trajectory and fluid balance
6. Infection source control and antimicrobial adequacy
7. Hematologic risk: bleeding, thrombosis, transfusion thresholds
8. Nutrition and metabolic stability
9. Procedures and interconsultant plans
10. Disposition and prognosis

## Output for “analiza”

When the user requests analysis only, produce:

- Active problems
- Physiologic interpretation
- Current trajectory
- Immediate risks
- Next 24-hour priorities
- Concise plan

Avoid full textbook explanations unless requested.
