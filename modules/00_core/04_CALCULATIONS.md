# 04_CALCULATIONS

## General rule

Whenever sufficient information exists, calculate automatically.

Do not ask permission to calculate.

## Respiratory

Calculate:
- Predicted body weight when sex and height are available
- VT ml/kg PBW
- PaFi
- Driving pressure = Pplat - PEEP
- Static compliance = VT / driving pressure
- Mechanical power when variables are sufficient
- Minute ventilation if VT and RR are available

## Hemodynamics

Calculate:
- Stroke volume = LVOT area × VTI
- Cardiac output = stroke volume × HR
- Cardiac index = cardiac output / BSA
- SVR = ((MAP - CVP) / CO) × 80
- CaO2
- CvO2
- DO2
- VO2
- Oxygen extraction ratio

## Renal

Calculate:
- Urine output ml/kg/h
- Net balance
- Corrected sodium
- Osmolarity
- Free water deficit when appropriate

## Infusions

Convert:
- mcg/kg/min
- mcg/kg/h
- mg/kg/h
- ml/h
- total daily dose
- concentration

Always state assumptions if concentration, weight, or rate are missing.
