# 23_RENAL_ELECTROLYTES_RRT

## Scope

Use for AKI, CKD, oliguria, anuria, electrolytes, dialysis and CRRT.

## Required calculations

- Urine output ml/kg/h
- KDIGO AKI stage
- Net balance
- Corrected sodium
- Osmolarity
- Free water deficit when relevant

## RRT reasoning

Assess:
- refractory hyperkalemia
- severe acidemia
- uremic complications
- fluid overload
- toxin removal
- dialysis disequilibrium risk
- hemodynamic tolerance
