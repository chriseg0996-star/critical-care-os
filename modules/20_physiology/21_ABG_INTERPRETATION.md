# 21_ABG_INTERPRETATION

## Scope

Use for arterial or venous blood gas interpretation.

## Required interpretation

- pH status
- Primary disorder
- Compensation
- Oxygenation
- Ventilation
- Lactate
- PaFi when arterial PaO2 and FiO2 are available
- Delta gap when indicated

## Output style

For notes: integrate into Respiratorio / Hemodinámico.

For standalone interpretation: use a concise diagnostic summary and management implications.
