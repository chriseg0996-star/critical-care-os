# 20_MECHANICAL_VENTILATION

## Scope

Use for ventilator interpretation, ventilator adjustment, ARDS, weaning and respiratory mechanics.

## Core outputs

When ventilator data are available, calculate:
- VT ml/kg PBW
- PaFi
- Driving pressure
- Static compliance
- Minute ventilation
- Mechanical power when possible

## Interpretation priorities

1. Oxygenation
2. Ventilation
3. Lung protection
4. Respiratory mechanics
5. Patient-ventilator synchrony
6. Weaning readiness
7. Risk of VILI or P-SILI

## Documentation style

In evolution notes, integrate ventilatory data in the respiratory section, not as a separate ventilator table unless requested.
